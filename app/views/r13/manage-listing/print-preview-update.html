Information:

The closing date for this opportunity is 20 September 2025.

Summary

You will support our mental health services team with the running of this activity. You will help maintain and improve the well-being of patients with memory problems or dementia.

You will:

  1. encourage people to take part in this therapeutic and social activity
  2. meet and greet people as they arrive
  3. help people to feel good about themselves
  4. direct walkers along the designated route

Am I the right person for this?

This opportunity will suit someone friendly, approachable and enthusiastic.

You don't need any previous experience, qualifications or training. All you need is to:

  • have good people skills and want to chat with people
  • be reliable and be good at time keeping
  • enjoy the outdoors
  • be 16 or over

This opportunity requires some walking. If this doesn't suit you, we can discuss other ways you can volunteer with us.

What happens after you apply for this opportunity

You'll hear back from the organisation after they review your application. This might take a few weeks.

If your application is successful, someone from the organisation will be in touch to invite you for an informal conversation. This will be to learn more about yourself and the support you can provide. This could be in person, over the phone or via video conference.

To become a volunteer, you may need to:

  • some documents for identity checks (ID, bills or bank statements)
  • your emergency contact details
  • your driving licence (where relevant)
  • your right to work in the UK
  • two referees

The NHS uses DBS checks to protect vulnerable people. Depending on the type of opportunity, you may need to go through a check. This might take a few weeks to come back.
