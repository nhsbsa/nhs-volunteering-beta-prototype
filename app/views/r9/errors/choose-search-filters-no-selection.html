Guidance

Search filters help users find more specific results.

Select any of options below which are relevant to your opportunity to help volunteers find your listing.

For example, if you needed someone to help tidy the gardens of a hospital on the weekend you might select 'Healthcare facility', 'Gardening and DIY' and 'Weekend' for your filters.

The volunteer won't see how you define it, it only helps them in finding relevant opportunities.
