Apply for a volunteering opportunity

Use this form to apply for a volunteering opportunity with an NHS organisation based in England.

Information:

You won't need register your details to apply.

We will only use your email address to contact you about this application.

Before you start

We will ask you for:

  • your personal and contact details
  • your availabilty
  • a statement about your reasons for volunteering

We might also ask the details of:

  • your emergency contact
  • your driving license (where relevant)
  • your right to work in the UK
  • two referees

The application should take you around 15 minutes to complete. You must fill out the application in a single session. You won't be able to save your progress.

If you need help with doing things online

If you prefer to apply using other methods, volunteering teams can offer paper forms or help you complete the online form.

Go back to get the contact details of the volunteering service team at your chosen organisation. You can discuss with them what support is available.
