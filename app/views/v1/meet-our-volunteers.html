Emma

Why I volunteer: when I retired, I wanted to keep active and help people in the community. Volunteering at the hospital was my first choice.

My typical day: I volunteer as a meet and greet at Sheffield Teaching Hospital. I welcome patients and visitors to the hospital and help them with directions to departments. I sometimes sit with patients who are waiting to returning home.

My one advice to you is: Give it a go, there are so many different opportunities available. It’s a great way of feeling you make a difference.
