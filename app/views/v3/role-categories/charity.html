Support NHS charities and fundraising

You will help with fundraising activities and events for NHS charities.

Opportunities you might find

  • Fundraiser
  • Charity shop or café volunteer
  • Charity support volunteer

What your activities could include

  • Working with the team to promote fundraising initiatives and ideas
  • Assisting with the running of the shop or café by handling the till and providing general customer service
  • Helping at events to raise funds for NHS charities

Consider these opportunities if you

  • Want to be closer to particular medical cause or projects
  • Enjoy direct contact with people sharing similar experiences
  • Have hands-on and a can-do attitude
What you will not have to do

You may be in direct contact with patients but you will not engage in any clinical or therapeutic activities.

You will not have to perform any tasks that require any medical expertise or training. Those tasks will remain under the responsibility of trained health and social care professionals.
