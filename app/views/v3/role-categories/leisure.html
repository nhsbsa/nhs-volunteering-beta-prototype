Assist with patient entertainment

You'll assist with the delivery of recreational activities for the well-being of patients.

Opportunities that might interest you

  • Hospital radio volunteer
  • Gardening volunteer
  • Arts and crafts activities volunteer
  • Patient wellbeing volunteer
  • Volunteer musician

What your tasks could include

  • Playing games with patients
  • Engage patients in nail painting, drawing or other activities
  • Encouraging patients to take part in arts and crafts activities
  • Helping maintain the garden areas around the site
  • Running the radio station to provide music and general entertainment

Consider these opportunities if you

  • Enjoy direct contact with people from diverse backgrounds
  • Are not an expert in arts and crafts but you enjoy them
  • Want to use your good communication and interpersonal skills
What you will not have to do

You may be in direct contact with patients but you will not engage in any clinical or therapeutic activities.

You will not have to perform any tasks that require any medical expertise or training. Those tasks will remain under the responsibility of trained health and social care professionals.
