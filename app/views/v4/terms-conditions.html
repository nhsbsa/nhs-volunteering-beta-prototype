Communication

When using the NHS volunteering service you must provide a valid personal email address each time you:

  • register your details for potential opportunities
  • apply for specific volunteering opportunities

If you supply an incorrect email address you will not receive messages related to your applications.

If you provide a telephone number then recruiting organisations may contact you by phone about your application.

We do not guarantee reliability of email communications. You should contact the recruiting organisation if you have any concerns.

Applications to volunteer using this service

If a recruiting organisation chooses to receive applications through this service, then you will need to complete a form through NHS Volunteering in order to apply. Our privacy policy explains how we collect, use and store your data on NHS Volunteering.

We will collect the information you provide in this form and share it with the relevant recruiting organisation.

The information you provide in your application form must be truthful, complete and accurate.

The information must not be:

  • false
  • misleading
  • offensive
  • discriminatory
  • threatening
  • abusive

The information in your application must not cause anxiety or distress. It must not encourage violence, hatred, or blasphemy. It must not be pornographic or breach confidence or privacy. It must not be unlawful, harassing, libellous, harmful, vulgar, obscene, or objectionable.

The declaration you agree to on submission of the form is valid and binding.

Submitting your details outside of this service

Recruiting organisations will assess all applications and will be responsible for all decisions relating to the volunteering opportunities.

A recruiting organisation may ask you to:

  • provide additional information about yourself
  • complete further screening processes for the role you have applied for

Instead of asking you to complete an application using this service, some recruiting organisations will provide a link to a third party website which you need to follow to apply. This will take you outside of NHS Volunteering.

When a recruiting organisation asks you for additional information or provides a link to a third party website to apply, any information you provide will not be subject to these terms and conditions, our privacy notice or cookies policy. Please refer to information provided by the recruiting organisation to understand how they will handle your data.

If you have questions about a volunteering opportunity or your application, you should contact the recruiting organisation.
