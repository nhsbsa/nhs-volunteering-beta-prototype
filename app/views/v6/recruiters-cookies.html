Cookies

This website is run by NHS Business Services Authority (NHSBSA) on behalf of NHS England and will be referred to as ‘we’ from now on.

Cookies are small files saved on your phone, tablet or computer when you visit a website.

NHS Volunteering uses cookies to:

  • make NHS Volunteering work, for example by keeping it secure
  • remember which pop-ups you've seen so they do not reappear
  • measure how you use our website, such as which pages you visit

We will not use cookies to identify you personally.

When you first visit us, you'll see a message about cookies that need to be stored on your computer. We also uses optional cookies which you can opt out of.

Find out how to manage cookies (opens in a new tab)
