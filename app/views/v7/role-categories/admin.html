Assist with admin tasks

You'll assist staff with general office tasks and with the smooth running of a department.

Opportunities you might find

  • Ward admin support
  • Admin and clerical support

What your activities could include

  • Entering data, filing and scanning documents
  • Making calls, answering the phone and taking messages
  • Dealing with patient enquiries
  • Transporting patient information around the site
  • Distributing and opening post

Consider these opportunities if you

  • Enjoy being part of a team
  • Are reliable and have a good understanding of confidentiality
  • Want to use your admin skills to help others
What you will not have to do

You may be in direct contact with patients but you will not engage in any clinical or therapeutic activities.

You will not have to perform any tasks that require any medical expertise or training. Those tasks will remain under the responsibility of trained health and social care professionals.
